# Quickstart: Esc Stop Playback

1. Start the app and trigger a spoken assistant response.
2. While the audio is playing, press **Esc**.
3. Confirm the spoken audio stops immediately.
4. Confirm the assistant message still remains visible in the conversation.
5. If additional spoken replies were queued, confirm they do not continue automatically.
6. Press **Esc** again while no audio is playing and confirm nothing breaks.

## Verification

- Spoken playback stops on Esc.
- Visible transcript remains on screen.
- Idle Esc does not create an error or change the conversation.
