# Quickstart: Recorder-Free Voice Capture

1. Install project dependencies.
2. Ensure SoX is not required on the machine.
3. Start Talk-Pi on Windows, macOS, or Linux.
4. Trigger voice recording with the normal voice shortcut.
5. Speak a short phrase and stop recording.
6. Confirm an audio recording is produced and the next voice step still works.
7. Repeat the flow a few times to confirm capture stays stable.
8. Simulate missing microphone access and confirm a clear error appears.

## Expected Results

- Voice recording starts without separate recorder setup.
- Recording stops cleanly.
- The produced audio still feeds the existing voice flow.
- Capture failures are visible and recoverable.
