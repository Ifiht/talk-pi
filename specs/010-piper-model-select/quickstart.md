# Quickstart: Voice Language Selection

## Verify voice language selection

1. Start Talk-Pi.
2. Open `/talk-pi`.
3. Confirm current Voice Language is shown.
4. Select a different Voice Language.
5. Trigger a spoken reply and confirm the new voice is used.
6. Speak in the selected language and confirm Whisper transcribes accordingly.
7. Reopen `/talk-pi` and confirm the active choice is still visible.

## Verify English voice

1. Open `/talk-pi`.
2. Choose English voice.
3. Trigger another spoken reply.
4. Confirm the assistant uses English voice output.
5. Speak in English and confirm Whisper follows the selected language.
6. Restart Talk-Pi and confirm the choice remains available or active.

## Expected outcome

- Voice choice is easy to find in `/talk-pi`.
- English voice is a first-class menu option.
- Missing model cases show a clear fallback or error.
